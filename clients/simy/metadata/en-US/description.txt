Simy is the modern platform for driving schools in Switzerland.

As a student, you can:
• Easily book driving lessons online
• Track your learning progress
• Communicate directly with your instructor
• View exam results
• Manage payments and invoices

For instructors and driving schools:
• Manage appointment calendars
• Overview of students and progress
• Exam planning and statistics
• Simple invoicing

Simy works with selected driving schools in Switzerland. Your access is set up directly by your driving school.
